The Heart of Little Hill

Little Hill Crochet is a collective of women from Kodaikanal, a serene hill town in Tamil Nadu. With limited employment opportunities, we came together to learn and create handmade crochet pieces. Through this, we empower each other, earn a sustainable income, and keep the art of crochet alive.
